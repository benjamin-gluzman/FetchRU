Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

Information We Collect

FetchRU stores your Discord User ID, watched Rutgers course indices, and application data necessary to provide notifications. We do not collect passwords, payment information, or the contents of your Discord messages.

How We Use Information

  • Monitor requested Rutgers course sections.
  • Send Discord notifications when a watched course opens.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service.

Data Sharing

FetchRU does not sell or share personal information except when required by law.

Data Retention

Watch data is retained only as long as needed to provide the Service and is removed when you delete it or after a successful notification.

Security

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no electronic system is completely secure.

Third‑Party Services

FetchRU uses Discord and Rutgers public course information. Their terms and privacy policies also apply.

Your Rights

You may stop using the Service at any time. To request deletion of stored information, contact your project email.

Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of FetchRU indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
